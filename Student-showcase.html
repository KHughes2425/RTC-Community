RealTime Community Education Special Interest Group Student Showcase 2024

Project Title:

"Virtual Production Showcase: Bringing Dreams to Life in Real Time"


Project Description:

The RealTime Community Education Special Interest Group is excited to announce the "Virtual Production Showcase 2024," an opportunity for students to demonstrate their skills and creativity by utilizing real-time technologies, with a focus on virtual production. This showcase provides a platform for students to apply their knowledge and showcase their projects to a broader audience.

Project Objective:

The primary goal of the Virtual Production Showcase is to foster innovation and practical application of real-time technologies and virtual production tools while encouraging students to push the boundaries of their creativity.

Project Details:
  1. Eligibility:
    • The showcase is open to students and recent graduates interested in real-time technologies, virtual production, 3D modeling, animation, game design, and related fields.

  2. Submission Categories: Students can submit projects in various categories, such as:
    • Real-Time Animation: Showcase animated characters or scenes created in real-time.
    • Virtual Film Production: Create a short virtual film using real-time tools.
    • Game Development: Develop a real-time interactive game or experience.
    • Immersive Environment: Design virtual environments using real-time technology.

  3. Submission Requirements:
    • A Project Description: Explain the concept, tools used, and the technology's real-time aspects.
    • Demonstration Video: Record a video demonstrating the project in action.
    • Documentation Provide documentation on the tools and technologies used.

  4. Evaluation and Prizes
    • A panel of judges from the RealTime Community will evaluate submissions based on innovation, creativity, and the effective use of real-time technology.
    • Prizes will be awarded to the best projects in each category, including certificates, educational resources, and potential networking opportunities within the real-time industry.

How to get involved


Students interested in participating in the Virtual Production Showcase can register and receive detailed guidelines for submission through the RealTime Community Education Special Interest Group's dedicated webpage. The showcase offers an excellent opportunity for students to demonstrate their skills, gain recognition, and connect with industry professionals. It also serves as a platform to inspire and engage the broader community in the exciting realm of real-time technologies and virtual production. This project aims to highlight the creative potential and practical applications of real-time technologies, fostering talent and innovation within the RealTime Community.
